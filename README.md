# 🎲 Lot Stacker

**"Stop guessing your liquidation. Stack lots, calculate risks, and survive the wild charts."**

[🇹🇭 อ่านภาษาไทยที่นี่ (Thai Version)](./README_TH.md)

**Lot Stacker** is a lightweight, real-time risk management utility built for forex/commodities traders (especially XAUUSD scalpers) who utilize **scale-in, pyramiding, or snowballing** strategies. 

No more guessing where your margin call or stop-out level sits. **Lot Stacker** mathematically tracks your combined risk profile every time you prepare to stack another position.

## 🔥 Why Lot Stacker?
In highly volatile markets, adding multiple positions (pyramiding) based on "gut feeling" is a shortcut to a blown account. This tool eliminates emotion by translating your next potential move into cold, hard data. Know your exact drawdown buffer before you execute.

## ✨ Core Features
- 🛡️ **Dynamic Risk Engine:** Calculates your maximum risk capital based on your current balance and broker's stop-out percentage.
- ➕ **Infinite Scaling:** Simulate adding unlimited trading positions; total lot size updates instantly.
- 🎯 **Auto Average Price:** Instantly calculates your combined break-even point without needing a separate calculator.
- 🚨 **Stop Out Radar:** Dynamically monitors your "Death Zone" (Stop-out price) and provides visual color warnings if your remaining drawdown buffer gets too tight.

## 🚀 Quick Start
No servers, no databases. Runs entirely in your local browser:
1. Download the `index.html` file.
2. Double-click to open it in Chrome, Safari, or Edge.
3. **Pro Tip:** Deploy it via **GitHub Pages** to access your personal calculator directly from your mobile phone while trading on the go.

## 🛠️ Tech Stack
- Vanilla JavaScript (Fast & Lightweight)
- HTML5
- Tailwind CSS (Dark-mode aesthetic designed for late-night trading sessions)
